# Task 02 – Systems of Equations

## Problem Statement

$$
2x+3y=12
$$

$$
x-y=1
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

From second equation

$$
x=y+1
$$

Substitute

$$
2(y+1)+3y=12
$$

$$
5y+2=12
$$

$$
y=2
$$

$$
x=3
$$

## Final Result

$$
x=3
$$

$$
y=2
$$

## Interpretation
Solution corresponds to intersection of two lines.
