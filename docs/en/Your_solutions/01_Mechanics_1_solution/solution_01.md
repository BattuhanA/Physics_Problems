# Task 01 – Projectile Motion

## Problem Statement

A projectile is fired with initial velocity $v_0 = 100 \text{ m/s}$ at angle $\theta = 37^\circ$.

## Theory

Motion under constant gravity:

$$
a_x = 0, \qquad a_y = -g
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

Velocity components:

$$
v_{0x} = v_0 \cos \theta
$$

$$
v_{0y} = v_0 \sin \theta
$$

Time of flight:

$$
T = \frac{2 v_{0y}}{g}
$$

Maximum height:

$$
H = \frac{v_{0y}^2}{2g}
$$

Range:

$$
R = \frac{v_0^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g}
$$

## Final Result

Formulas above.

## Interpretation

Projectile follows parabolic motion under constant gravity.
