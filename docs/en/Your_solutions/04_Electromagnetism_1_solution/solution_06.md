# ⚡ Task 6 – Electric Field

## 🔹 Formula

\[
\vec{E} = k \sum \frac{q_i \vec{r}_i}{r_i^3}
\]

---

## 🔹 At (0,y)

\[
r = \sqrt{a^2 + y^2}
\]

---

## 🔹 Components

\[
E_x = kq \frac{a}{r^3}
\]

\[
E_y = \frac{3kqy}{r^3}
\]

---

## 🔹 Limit (y >> a)

\[
E \approx \frac{3kq}{y^2}
\]

---

## ✅ Insight

👉 Far distance → behaves like single charge
