# ⚡ Task 1 – Coulomb's Law

## 🧲 Problem
Four point charges of +1 C are placed at the corners of a square (1 m).  
Find the force on a -2 C charge at the center.

---

## 🔹 Step 1: Distance

\[
r = \frac{a}{\sqrt{2}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}
\]

---

## 🔹 Step 2: Force from one charge

\[
F = k \frac{|qQ|}{r^2}
= 9\times10^9 \cdot \frac{2}{1/2}
= 36\times10^9 \, N
\]

---

## 🔹 Step 3: Symmetry ⚖️

- Forces cancel in x-direction ❌  
- Forces cancel in y-direction ❌  

---

## ✅ Final Answer

\[
\boxed{F_{net} = 0}
\]

👉 Perfect symmetry → zero net force
