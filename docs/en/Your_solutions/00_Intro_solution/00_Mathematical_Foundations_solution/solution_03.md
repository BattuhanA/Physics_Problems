# Task 03 – Proportionality

## Problem Statement

$$
F = G \frac{m_1 m_2}{r^2}
$$

Masses halved, distance doubled.

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
m_1' = m_1/2
$$

$$
m_2' = m_2/2
$$

$$
r' = 2r
$$

$$
F' = G\frac{(m_1/2)(m_2/2)}{(2r)^2}
$$

$$
F' = G\frac{m_1 m_2}{16r^2}
$$

## Final Result

$$
F' = F/16
$$

## Interpretation
Force becomes sixteen times smaller.
