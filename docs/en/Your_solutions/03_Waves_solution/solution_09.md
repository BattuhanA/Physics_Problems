# Solution 09 - Damped Oscillator

## Equation

m x'' + b x' + kx = 0

## General Solution

Depends on damping:

### Underdamped

b² < 4mk

### Critical

b² = 4mk

### Overdamped

b² > 4mk

## Numerical Method

RK4 used for simulation
