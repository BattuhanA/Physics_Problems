# Solution 03 – Pendulum Speed

## Given:
- Length: L = 1.0 m  
- Initial angle: θ = 15°  
- g = 9.8 m/s²  

---

## Step 1: Height Difference

h = L(1 - cosθ)

h = 1(1 - cos15°)

cos15° ≈ 0.966

h ≈ 1(1 - 0.966) = 0.034 m

---

## Step 2: Energy Conservation

Potential → Kinetic

mgh = (1/2)mv²

---

## Step 3: Solve for velocity

v = √(2gh)

v = √(2 × 9.8 × 0.034)

v ≈ √(0.6664)

v ≈ 0.82 m/s

---

## Final Answer:
v ≈ 0.82 m/s✅
